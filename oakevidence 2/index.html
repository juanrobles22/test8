Built for the whole street

One network. Four very different jobs.

A property manager, an HOA board, a patrol officer, and a homeowner all need different things from a camera. OakEvidence gives each of them their own front door to the same network.

Protect the lot, not just the building.

Parking lots, loading docks, and back entrances are where most loss happens — and where fixed cameras see the least. OakEvidence covers the perimeter, not just the door.

  • Continuous plate & vehicle capture across the property line
  • Instant lookback after a break-in, theft, or dispute
  • One-click evidence export for insurance or police reports

Give the whole block the same set of eyes.

Every resident chips in coverage, every resident benefits from it. The board sets the rules; OakEvidence handles the network, storage, and sharing.

  • Shared entry & exit coverage across the neighborhood
  • Board-level controls over who can search and for how long
  • Simple resident opt-in for driveway or porch cameras

Evidence that's already organized when you arrive.

Agencies get a single, searchable view across every connected business and neighborhood that's opted in — with a clear chain of custody for everything pulled.

  • Plate, color, make, and pattern search across the whole network
  • Audit-logged access for every case, every request
  • Community-controlled sharing — agencies never see opted-out feeds

The same network, sized for a front porch.

Homeowners don't need a pole-mounted sensor array. OakEvidence Home is a single peel-and-stick camera that taps into the same neighborhood network — only the hardware changes.

  • 10-minute self-install, no wiring or subscription required to start
  • Opt-in sharing with your street, HOA, or local police — never automatic
  • Plain-language alerts instead of raw footage to dig through
Under the hood

What the network actually catches.

Plates are only part of the picture. OakEvidence is built to recognize what's moving through a street even when a plate is missing, swapped, or out of frame.

License plate recognition

Reads plates day or night, across multiple lanes, at normal driving speed.

Vehicle fingerprinting

Identifies make, type, and color even when a plate is covered or missing.

Real-time alerts

Flags a watch-listed plate or pattern the moment it crosses a camera.

Searchable archive

Filter weeks of footage by plate, vehicle type, color, or time in seconds.

Secure sharing

Send a clip or case file to an agency without handing over your whole feed.

Full audit log

Every search and export is logged — who looked, when, and why.
